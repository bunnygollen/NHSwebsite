Volunteer
All NHS members are required to complete 25 service hours per semester from at least two different events/organizations (Check the guidelines for more info). Below are listed a few volunteer opportunities nearby. If you have any suggestions to share, contact an officer or email us at clementsnationalhonorsociety@gmail.com.

Remember to look for ongoing TSP's for volunteer opportunities! (Check the Clements NHS Facebook group)
Ongoing Opportunities
Clements Softball Concessions
  • Help run the concession stand at softball games (about 4 hours per session)
  • Running from February to April during softball season
  • Check the sign-up to see if spots are still available
Second Mile Mission Center
  • Sort canned foods, clothing, and books
  • Located in Stafford, TX
  • Call 281-261-9199 or e-mail volunteer@secondmile.org.
Houston Food Bank
  • Help to package food for families in need
  • Visit this link or call 713-223-3700 for more information
